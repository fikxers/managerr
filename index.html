Gated Resedential Communities and Estates

Our primary focus is on gated residential communities and housing estates, where residents seek a serene and secure living environment. These communities often consist of single-family homes or townhouses surrounded by perimeter walls or fences, offering an exclusive and private atmosphere.

Condominiums and Apartment Complexes

In urban areas, apartments and condominiums represent a significant segment of the housing market. These multi-unit serviced complexes require efficient management to cater to a diverse range of residents. For residents, it simplifies maintenance requests, facilitates communication, and fosters a strong sense of community.

Commercial / Recreational Facilities

These spaces encompass business parks, commercial estates, and recreational areas, where efficient facility management is essential to creating a positive experience for employees, visitors, and patrons. Haiven equips facility managers in these spaces with powerful tools to manage access control, handle billing and financial operations, and engage with stakeholders effectively.
